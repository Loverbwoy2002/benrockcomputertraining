CODE OF ETHICS

Our commitment to excellence, discipline, and community values that shape our institution's culture

Dismissal Policy

Irregular attendance, habitual idleness, disobedience or malpractice in connection with examinations, causes injury to the moral tone of the school and is a sufficient reason for the dismissal of the student.

Attendance Policy

  • No student shall be absent from class without sufficient reason
  • Parents/Guardians must clearly state the reason for the absence
  • Students are not allowed to go out during class hours
  • Those who absent themselves without prior permission shall not enter the class without the principal's permission
  • Unauthorized absence for more than 5 days will lead to removal from the rolls

School Uniform

The School Lacoste is obligatory on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays and official functions. It is the duty of the parents to see that the student comes to school neatly dressed in the prescribed uniform.

Discipline

Discipline is an integral part of the learning process. To develop self-control and to live in harmony with others, respect family traditions and keep the laws of the Institution.

  • Good customer relationship is compulsory within the Institution
  • Every student shall endeavor to make the school proud by excelling in studies & good manners
  • Politeness, courtesy in speech & conduct as well as cleanliness and grace of person are to be cherished

Parental Co-operation

Parents/Guardians are not allowed to enter the classrooms. They can meet the teachers only after obtaining permission from the principal.

Academic Integrity

All students are expected to maintain the highest standards of academic honesty. Plagiarism, cheating, or any form of academic dishonesty will not be tolerated.

  • Always cite sources properly
  • Complete all assignments independently unless group work is specified
  • Report any incidents of academic dishonesty to faculty
