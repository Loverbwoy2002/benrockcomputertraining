About Benrock Computer Training Centre

Located in Opah-Sarpeiman, Accra, Benrock Computer Training Centre has been providing quality computer education and training services. We specialize in micro-computer training, consultancy, sales, secretariate training, and graphic designing.

Our mission is to equip students with practical computer skills that make them job-ready and capable of establishing themselves professionally. We train you to become a professional who can excel in any firm or start your own business.
